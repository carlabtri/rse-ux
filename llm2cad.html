LLM2CAD

A tool that transforms text, image, and program prompts into CAD files — bridging human creativity and precision engineering through AI-powered generation.

▶ Demo✦ Figma⬡ Jira Epic

Overview

LLM2CAD transforms text, image, and program prompts into CAD files. Users describe what they want — in natural language, as an image reference, or as a program — and the tool produces precise CAD output in industry-standard BREP geometry.

Research Context

Built by researchers and software developers from RSE for the Human-Centered AI / Future Product Innovation (HCAI) team.

Input Flexibility

Accepts text descriptions, image references, and program-based specifications — giving engineers and designers multiple ways to communicate their intent to the system.

BREP Geometry Output

All inputs are converted into industry-standard Boundary Representation (BREP) geometry, ensuring outputs are compatible with professional CAD workflows and manufacturing pipelines.

AI Vision & Language Models

Advanced vision and language models interpret engineering intent from natural language and visual references, generating manufacturable 3D models without manual reconstruction.

Natural Language Input Form

An intuitive prompt interface designed to minimize the learning curve — describe what you need in plain language and the model handles the translation to geometry.

CAD Viewer

A built-in 3D viewer with exploration controls lets users inspect generated geometry before exporting, with tools for rotation, pan, and zoom.

Feedback Loop

A direct connection between the prompt and output panels supports rapid iteration — refine your description and regenerate geometry in a tight design loop.
