LLM2CAD

A tool bridging human creativity and precision engineering through AI-powered CAD generation, featuring an enhanced input form and CAD viewer experience.

▶ Demo✦ Figma⬡ Jira Epic

Overview

LLM2CAD bridges the gap between human creativity and precision engineering through AI-powered CAD generation. Users describe what they want in natural language and the tool produces CAD output.

Design Focus

  • Intuitive natural language input form
  • Clear CAD viewer with exploration controls
  • Feedback loop between prompt and output
