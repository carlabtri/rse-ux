Unstuck

Ubiquitous Just-in-Time Interventions for Creative Professionals — a browser-based app helping people overcome creative blocks at the right moment.

✦ Figma📊 Slides⬡ Jira Story

Overview

Unstuck provides just-in-time interventions for creative professionals experiencing blocks — delivering contextual nudges and creative prompts at the right moment in the workflow.

Context

A browser-based application targeting creative professionals who benefit from external stimuli when stuck in ideation or problem-solving phases.
