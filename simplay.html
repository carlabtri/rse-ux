Simplay

A driving simulation and feedback platform that communicates performance scores and actionable feedback in a clear, motivating experience.

▶ Demo✦ Figma⬡ Jira Epic

Overview

A driving simulation and feedback platform. The design communicates driving performance scores and actionable feedback in a clear, motivating experience for drivers.

Problem

Drivers needed a clear, motivating way to understand their performance after each simulation trip — without being overwhelmed by raw data.

Goals

  • Communicate trip scores clearly
  • Surface actionable driving feedback
  • Keep the experience rewarding, not punitive
