SWARM — Toyota Research Program (TRP)

A pitch for an optional program for Toyota employees and customers offering customized control over data sharing and unlocking real-world data for research and development.

Overview

An optional program for Toyota employees and customers offering customized control over data sharing and unlocking real-world data for research and development.

SWARM

Toyota's annual, global hackathon (since 2019) to design, build, and pitch technology ideas. 53 teams from over a dozen global Toyota regions compete in a 72-hour competition in Plano, TX, across 3 judging rounds.

Toyota Research Program

Nothing required to enter. By opting in, participants can selectively share data Toyota already has for additional R&D uses — giving researchers access to real-world driving data while keeping participants in full control of what they share.

Dashcam

Participants receive a dashcam in the mail to mount in their vehicle. The device captures and transmits driving video, enabling richer behavioral and environmental data collection for Toyota's research programs.

Offboarding

Participants can leave the program at any time. Upon exit, their data is removed from all research datasets — ensuring a transparent and fully reversible opt-in experience.
