SWARM Hackathon

An optional program for Toyota employees and customers offering customized control over data sharing and unlocking real-world data for research and development.

▶ Demo 📊 Slides ✦ Figma ▶ Pitch Video

Overview

An optional program designed for Toyota employees and customers, providing customized control over data sharing and how it is used by Toyota — while unlocking real-world data for research and development.

Key Features

  • Customized control over data sharing and usage
  • Unlocks real-world driving data for R&D
  • Transparent opt-in/opt-out experience
