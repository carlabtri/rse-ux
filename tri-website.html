TRI Website Redesign

End-to-end engagement covering UX research (stakeholder interviews, usability testing) followed by a full website redesign proposal informed by the findings.

▶ Prototype✦ Figma⬡ Jira Epic

Part 1 — UX Research

A comprehensive UX research engagement assessing quantitative, qualitative, attitudinal, and behavioral data to improve usability and user engagement on the TRI website.

Goals

The TRI website is underperforming as a strategic asset — this initiative goes beyond a cosmetic refresh.

  • Unlock missed opportunities in partnerships, recruiting, and brand positioning
  • Address clear barriers to engagement, usability, and perception identified through UX research and data analysis
  • Deliver findings, business implications, and actionable recommendations to elevate the site into a high-value digital asset

Part 2 — Website Redesign Proposal

A design proposal translating research insights into a cohesive visual and interaction design.
