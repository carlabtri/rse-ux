Unstuck

Ubiquitous Just-in-Time Interventions for Creative Professionals — a browser-based app helping people overcome creative blocks at the right moment.

▶ Demo✦ Figma⬡ Jira Story

Overview

Unstuck provides just-in-time (JIT) interventions for creative professionals experiencing blocks — delivering contextual nudges and creative prompts at the right moment in the workflow to foster sustained behavioral change.

Behavioral Framework

Grounded in a three-stage model: Existing Awareness → Action & Adaptation → Autonomy & Habit — progressing from identifying behavioral insights, to developing models of behavior, to improving behavior with technology.

Research Scope

A three-phase mixed-methods series: formative, longitudinal (S2: N = 225 creative professionals over 5 weeks), and follow-up — evaluating how JIT interventions help professionals self-regulate creative stuckness over time.

Research Outcomes

Evidence that micro-interventions improve creativity, wellbeing, and self-regulation across timescales — fostering sustained behavioral change in how creative professionals navigate stuckness.

Theoretical Foundation

A theoretically and empirically grounded definition of creative stuckness as a measurable, multifaceted psychological state — establishing a principled basis for intervention design and evaluation.

Three-Phase Research Series

A mixed-methods progression — formative, longitudinal (S2: N = 225 creative professionals over 5 weeks), and follow-up — showing how professionals learn to regulate stuckness through JIT interventions over time.

Ubiquitous Creativity Support

A browser-based system designed to fit naturally into existing workflows — promoting autonomy and long-term adaptive regulation without disrupting the creative process.
